About Greenshift Precast and Construction Ltd

Greenshift Precast and Construction Ltd is a professional contracting company based in Kenya, established in 2025. We are focused on delivering modern, affordable, and sustainable construction solutions using precast technology. We aim to redefine Kenya’s construction landscape by combining structural engineering innovation with landscape design expertise.

Mission & Vision
